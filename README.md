# MockupShot - Free Device Mockup Generator

[![MockupShot Screenshot](https://mockupshot.online/og-image.png)](https://mockupshot.online/)

**MockupShot** is a free online tool that adds realistic device frames to your screenshots. Perfect for product showcases, portfolios, and app presentations.

## ✨ Features

- **Browser Frames** — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge
- **Phone Frames** — iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, generic phone
- **Tablet Frames** — iPad
- **Device Frames** — MacBook, iMac
- **Drag & Drop** — Upload images instantly
- **Customizable** — Device color, background, shadow

## 🔒 Privacy First

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your screenshots never leave your device.
